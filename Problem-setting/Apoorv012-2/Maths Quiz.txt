                                                    Maths Quiz

You are participating in a mathematics and computation quiz. You get a question in which you would be given two numbers.

1. The number from which you would take the digits. You then make all the numbers possible out of it of varying size.
2. The number X.

Constraints: You can be sure that the first number would not have any repeating digit.

You have to tell how many numbers from the list, are divisible by X.
